About

I help organizations build design systems that ship, UX strategies tied to business outcomes, and AI-augmented workflows that amplify craft.

15+ years across enterprise SaaS, healthcare, and product design. I've led design teams, built design systems from scratch, and translated dense product strategy into shipping work.

What I believe

Design systems should model business domains, not UI primitives. The best design work happens when you understand the problem deeply enough to make it simple. AI is a lever, not a replacement — the best results come from designers who know how to use it as a thinking tool.

How I work

I'm based in Kansas City and work with teams anywhere. Currently independent, available for consulting engagements and senior design leadership roles.
