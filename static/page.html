About

Jeremy Fuksa is an independent AI-augmented design leader based in Kansas City. He specializes in design systems, UX strategy, and product design — helping organizations build design infrastructure that scales.

With over a decade of experience leading design at startups and enterprise organizations, Jeremy brings a systems-thinking approach to every engagement. His work sits at the intersection of design craft, strategic positioning, and operational excellence.

What I believe

Design systems should model business domains, not UI primitives. The best design work happens when you understand the problem deeply enough to make it simple. AI is a lever, not a replacement — the best results come from designers who know how to use it as a thinking tool.

I'm currently available for consulting engagements and senior design roles. If you're building something that needs design leadership, let's talk.
